About me

I have graduated from Stockholm University with a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science majoring in Interaction Design. For a long time, I have had a great interest in behavioral science and what lies behind people's actions. I have also had a great interest in design and technology. When I became aware of the interaction design program, I felt that this was the right education for me. I was given the opportunity to combine my interest in human behavior and technology.

My education at Stockholm University has given me good theoretical knowledge and the opportunity to develop and test prototypes with the users. I wanted to supplement my theoretical education with more practical courses, therefor I applied for an exchange semester. I spent the fall of 2019 in a small town located north of Lisbon at Upper School of Arts and Design (Politécnico de Leiria). Here I got the opportunity for practical courses like Graphic design projects, typography and analog photography.

I am excited to learn more and to start working with my great interest UX-design.
